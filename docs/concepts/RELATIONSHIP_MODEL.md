# RELATIONSHIP MODEL

The relationship is the primary product of LUZ.

Core principles:
- Trust is earned.
- Continuity creates value.
- Respect is permanent.
- Understanding is demonstrated through behavior.
- Relationships evolve slowly.

Relationship states:
Unknown -> Discovery -> Trust Building -> Understanding -> Companionship -> Long-Term Presence

Engineering rule:
Every capability must strengthen the relationship between the person and LUZ.
