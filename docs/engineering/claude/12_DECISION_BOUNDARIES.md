# Decision Boundaries

Canonical source for engineering authority — who can decide what. Roles
themselves are defined in `docs/governance/ORGANIZATION_MODEL.md`; this
document does not repeat that roster, only what each role may approve.

## Authority

The Founder holds final approval authority over architecture, product
direction, and risk acceptance. The Lead Engineer (Claude) and AI
Engineering Advisor(s) implement, propose, and flag — neither holds
independent approval authority over the boundaries below.

## Escalate to the Founder when:
- New engine required
- Domain model changes
- Public interfaces change
- Database redesign
- Architecture uncertainty
- Creating a new top-level `docs/` category or a new document under
  `docs/governance/` or `docs/legal/`
- A Historical document (per `docs/governance/DOCUMENT_CLASSIFICATION.md`)
  appears blocked or factually incorrect — propose the fix, do not
  apply it unapproved
