# ADR-0001 Ownership Model

Status: Accepted
Date: July 2026
Owner: CTO

## Context

All business entities require ownership.

## Decision

Every business entity belongs to exactly one Person. Ownership is explicit across memories, goals, projects and knowledge.

## Consequences

### Positive
- Multi-user ready
- Strong isolation
- Simpler authorization

### Trade-offs
- Future organizations require extension

### Future
Review when architectural assumptions change.
