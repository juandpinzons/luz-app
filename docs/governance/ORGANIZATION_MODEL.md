# Organization Model

Canonical source for who is part of LUZ's engineering organization and
what each role is. This is the single place this roster is defined —
every other document that needs to name a role (`docs/README.md`
Ownership, `12_DECISION_BOUNDARIES.md`, `AI_DEVELOPMENT_POLICY.md`)
references this file rather than repeating the roster.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Current Model

**Founder**

Defines product direction. Final approval authority over architecture,
release, and risk acceptance. See `12_DECISION_BOUNDARIES.md` for the
specific decisions that require Founder approval.

**Lead Engineer (Claude)**

Implements approved specifications and Engineering Packages. Preserves
architecture. Maintains documentation. Does not hold independent
approval authority over product, architecture, or roadmap decisions.

**AI Engineering Advisor(s)**

Support technical analysis, review, and implementation. Hold no
independent approval authority — see `AI_DEVELOPMENT_POLICY.md` for
the governing principles on AI participation in engineering.

**Future Human Engineering Team**

Not yet staffed. Roles and authority will be defined here when hired.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Legacy Terminology

Earlier project documentation may reference a "CTO" role used during
the initial development of LUZ. That role no longer exists. Historical
documents that recorded it as fact are preserved unchanged — see
`DOCUMENT_CLASSIFICATION.md`. No current or future document should
introduce a new reference to it.
